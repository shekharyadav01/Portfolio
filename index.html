About Me

I am Shekhar Yadav, a tech enthusiast from Ghiror, a small town near Mainpuri, Uttar Pradesh. I completed my schooling in Mainpuri and am currently pursuing a B.Tech in Information Technology from NIET, Greater Noida. My journey into technology began with a deep fascination for computers, and since then, I’ve dedicated myself to mastering the art of problem-solving through data and technology.
With a strong foundation in Python, SQL, Data Analytics, Machine Learning, and Cloud Computing, I specialize in transforming raw data into meaningful insights. I have hands-on experience with tools like Power BI and Excel, and my projects include data visualization, predictive modeling, and business intelligence solutions. These experiences have helped me develop a practical understanding of solving real-world problems through technology.
I’m passionate about learning, adapting to new challenges, and staying ahead in the ever-evolving tech industry. My goal is to grow as a skilled professional in the field of data science and analytics, contributing to innovative, data-driven solutions that make a meaningful impact.
